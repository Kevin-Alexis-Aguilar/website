Through the years, I've been built miniture cars, battleships, cannons, houses, and other creations. Recently, I improvised with my new vehicle and I ended up creating a steam truck that are from the early 1900s. If you view it from behind, it looks like a steam locomotive.

Note: the truck is not propelled by actual steam.

Speaking of vehicles, I made a crusier like vessel that measures 110 centimeters! It has a double barred turret in the bow, behind and above it, lays a lone thine turret. Within the middle lays two lifeboats on its side with the captain's bridge in the center. An anti- aircraft gun is placed on the rear end of the poopdeck. Finally, A small torpedo gun is located at the stern of my beloved ship.
